POST 02. encrypt
{{base_url}}/omnic/api/insuranceService/encrypt

The endpointencryptis a request to secure insurance service data.

Required:

  • userName: The username of the user.

  • password: The password of the user.

Validation Rules:

  • Date: The date should be in month/day/year format (e.g., 11/28/2024).

  • Car Registration No.: The car registration number must be no more than 11 characters in length.

  • Chasis Number: The chasis number must not contain the letters “I” and “O”.

The length of the chasis number must be no more than 17 characters.

  • Sum Insured Type: The sum insured should be numeric (i.e., a number)
